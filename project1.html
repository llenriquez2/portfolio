Incident Report Generator for NOC

Developed a tool to automate and improve the incident reporting process for corporate clients, streamlining the workflow and eliminating errors.

Objective

To enhance the incident reporting process for corporate clients by automating report creation and minimizing errors and redundancies.

Key Contributions

  • Designed a system where affected account numbers are entered into an input field, automatically populating relevant account details.
  • Integrated functionality to export detailed incident reports as PDF files, including signatures from the supervisor and manager.
  • Introduced a searchable database to retrieve fault numbers and generate reports without duplicate entries or time discrepancies.

Impact

The tool significantly reduced report duplication, eliminated time discrepancies, and improved the efficiency and accuracy of incident reporting for corporate clients.
