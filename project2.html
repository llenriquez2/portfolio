Network Activity Scheduler

Developed a tool to streamline the scheduling of network activities, reducing the coordination required with the NOC team.

Objective

Developed the Network Activity Scheduler to facilitate smoother management of network activities by enabling contractors to directly submit their scheduled tasks, minimizing the need for coordination with the NOC team.

Key Contributions

  • Created a system allowing contractors to input, manage, and monitor their network activities, pending approval from supervisors, reducing dependency on NOC for scheduling.
  • Integrated the system with NOC monitoring tools for seamless updates and real-time task visibility in the NAR tab.
  • Ensured that once activities are approved and connected to NOCDB, further updates or modifications are restricted to the NOC team.
  • Linked approved tasks directly to the NOC monitoring system, minimizing manual coordination and administrative overhead.

Impact

The system boosted task visibility, reduced errors, lowered the coordination burden on the NOC team, and significantly improved scheduling efficiency for network activities.
